If you have a link to an image, you can find the tweet it was from.

  • Take the identifier from the link. If the link is https://pbs.twimg.com/media/DsV_snjUUAAFyqg.jpg, the identifier is DsV_snjUUAAFyqg.
  • Press Ctrl+F to bring up the page search.
  • Enter the identifier. That will take you to the box with the image.
  • Click the [SRC] button.

When you save an image, make sure it has :orig at the end so you get it at full size. For example, https://pbs.twimg.com/media/DsV_snjUUAAFyqg.jpg:orig.
